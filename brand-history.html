Looking towards a span of 100 years.

SYRINX believes in values that accumulate over time,
rather than those rapidly created and consumed.

A place where a single record, a single book, and a single object
acquire new meaning through the passage of time.

Choosing essence over trend, and time over the fleeting moment,
we slowly accumulate the solid fragments of life.

Collected fragments of a constructive life.
